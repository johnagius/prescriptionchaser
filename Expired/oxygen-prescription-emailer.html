Oxygen Prescription — Email Sender

One email per recipient. Administrators with several patients get a single combined email. Click Open all visible to queue every compose tab.

Anyone emailed in the last 24 hours is skipped and shown greyed-out at the bottom — so you can safely re-run without double-sending (this is remembered even if you close the file). To avoid the browser blocking the burst, tabs open one every 0.6s; if any are blocked you'll get a warning and they stay marked un-sent. Best practice: allow pop-ups for this page, then use Open next 10 repeatedly. High importance isn't supported by Outlook web's compose link — set it manually via \u22ef \u2192 Set importance \u2192 High.
